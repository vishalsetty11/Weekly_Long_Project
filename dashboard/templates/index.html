Weekly Long: Medallion Architecture

Downloading

Automated scripts fetch daily Bhavcopy CSV files from the NSE website, saving raw data as CSV files in MotherDuck as IngestedFiles.

Bronze

Raw CSVs land in `bronze.bhavcopy_raw`, serving as the immutable landing zone for market data.

Silver

Data is cleaned; scripts calculate weekly closes and 180-day metrics, stored in specific Silver tables.

Gold

SQL joins merge breakout and surge tables, creating `gold.historical_signals` for high-conviction setups.

Display

Flask queries the Gold layer; frontend renders signals into this filterable, high-performance dashboard.
